About Monk.AI

Monk.AI is a decentralized artificial intelligence infrastructure protocol designed to power autonomous systems on blockchain networks.


By combining distributed neural computation, staking-based incentives, and governance-driven architecture, Monk.AI creates a scalable, permissionless AI ecosystem for developers, enterprises, and next-generation decentralized applications.


Our mission is to build the foundational AI layer for Web3 — secure, autonomous, and community-governed.
